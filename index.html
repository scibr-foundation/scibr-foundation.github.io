We DO

We build Knowledge Networks

We promote and work in partnership with local knowledge-based networks, connecting, supporting and helping publicize their activities.

PUBx Framework

PUB meetups are interdisciplinary seminars and networking, in which invited speakers present what they research, study, create and develop. They are organized by the Brazilian scientific, technology/innovation and cultural community in various locations.

Innovation Capacity

SciBr works with companies and scientists, both in the US and Brazil, to build the innovation ecosystem and drive projects forward. We train senior executives on the role and strategies of innovation as well as work with researchers meet market needs.

Talent Portfolio

Ultra-specialized high-skilled individuals are not easy to find. We keep track of over 7000 Brazilians that have or had an international experience in the sciences, innovation and arts.

SciBr Summit

The first research and innovation conference that spoke Portuguese at MIT started in 2013 and happens every 3 years. We use the SciBr Summit to bring top-notch brazilian talent to Boston for a massive celebration of their work.

Research Grants

One of our grantees, sponsored by the Lemann Foundation, took a Robotics course to a high school in the Amazon. Professors and students learned 3D printing, mechanical engineering and programming for the first time.

Advocacy

We support research and innovation in the public and private spheres. SciBr has met and trained congressmen, presidents, governors and senators, showing a path to a better Brazil through investments in research and development.

The Foundation

SciBr is a 501(c)(3) charitable organization dedicated to promoting meaningful cultural, educational and scientific exchange between the United States and Brazil.

Our mission is to connect Brazilian researchers, artists and entrepreneurs working abroad and to mobilize their talents to create a more prosperous, fair and sustainable Brazil
